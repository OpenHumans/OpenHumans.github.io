Open Humans Foundation

The Open Humans Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to enabling individuals to access their data and share it with research studies.

Programs

Open Humans

Open Humans is a platform that enables individuals to connect their data with research and citizen science. Visit www.openhumans.org to learn more!

Personal Genome Project

The Open Humans Foundation helps support the Global Network of Personal Genome Projects. These studies are dedicated to using an ethical "open consent" format to create public resources of genome data, health information, and cell lines.

GET Conference

Since 2010, we've also organized the GET Conference, an annual gathering that brings together leading thinkers to discuss how we measure and understand people and their traits.
